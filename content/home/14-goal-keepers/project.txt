Synopsis: German coach Adrian Haasner travels to Israel to teach Jewish and Palestinian boys how to play soccer together. The story reaches its climax at a tournament in an Arab city with over a thousand participating children. Soon, it becomes clear that this playful engagement has the potential for solving fundamental problems.
